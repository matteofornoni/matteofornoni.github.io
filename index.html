ABOUT ME

I am currently a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Pavia, under the supervision of prof. Elisabetta Rocca. My research focuses on the mathematical analysis of Partial Differential Equations, mostly of evolutionary type, and partly on Calculus of Variations. As my PhD project, I am studying analytical properties of mathematical models concerning biological phenomena such as tumour growth. In particular, I study systems of PDEs of Cahn-Hilliard or Allen-Cahn type and tackle problems regarding well-posedness, regularity, long-time behaviour, optimal control and inverse identification of initial data for these models.
